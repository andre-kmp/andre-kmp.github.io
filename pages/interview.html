Rarely Asked Questions

July 2026.

What is your favorite holiday?

Corpus Christi. It is a long holiday and there are no celebrations to attend.

What is your favorite website?

Currently, Semantris. It is a word association game by Google. If you play Arcade mode and would like to submit your scores to my leaderboard, here is the link.

What is your favorite season?

Spring. Not so hot, not so rainy.

What movie do you wish you could watch again for the first time?

Ex-Machina.

What is the best advice you have ever received?

"Be very drastic about cutting off, and be very intentional about what you want to include".

For what in your life do you feel most grateful?

Pragmatically, to have had the opportunity to study English as a teenager. Today, I have access to more and higher-quality information than I would if I spoke only Portuguese.

What is your definition of success?

Fully freedom to decide how to spend time (at least free time).

Do you believe in free will?

Yes.

Do you believe in destiny?

Also yes.

Where do you think we go when we die?

Either we move on to a new collective dimension (what spiritualists call the spiritual world), or to an eternal and individual experience similar to a dream.

Do you work better in the morning or at night?

Both: early morning or late night.

How do you take your coffee?

Warm and with no sugar. I don't enjoy extremely hot coffee, always wait 10 to 15 min to take it.

If you lived 500 years ago, what do you think your profession would be?

If I were in the same location I am today, I hope no profession. If I were in Europa, Africa or Asia, I think I would be a monk or a librarian.

What career advice would you give to your younger self?

Take it easy.

Do you play any instruments?

Yes. Acoustic guitar.

Do you play any sports?

Not yet.

If you could choose one superpower: Flight or Invisibility?

Flight.

Do you pay more attention to the lyrics or the instrumentation?

Instrumentation first, always. I also value instrumentation more: I like many songs with 5/10 lyrics and 10/10 instrumentation, but rarely the other way around.

Do you prefer sweet or savory snacks?

Mostly savory.
